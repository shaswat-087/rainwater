Rooftop Rainwater Harvesting System

Estimate • Harvest • Conserve

Introduction

Rooftop Rainwater Harvesting is a method of collecting and storing rainwater from rooftops for future use. It helps in conserving water, reducing water bills, and improving groundwater levels.

Step-by-Step Installation guide

Step 1: Catchment Preparation

Clean the rooftop surface, ensure gutters are intact.

Step 2: Gutters & Downpipes

Install PVC/metal channels to direct water.

Step 3: First Flush Diverter

Add a mechanism to discard initial dirty rainwater.

Step 4: Filtration Unit

Use sand/charcoal/mesh filters to remove debris.

Step 5: Storage Tank

Place above-ground or underground tanks sized to demand.

Step 6: Recharge pit/well

Optional, for directing excess water into aquifers.

Step 7: Distribution System

Connect tank to household taps or irrigation lines.

Benefits of Rainwater Harvesting

  • Conserves water resources
  • Reduces water bills
  • Increases groundwater levels
  • Eco-friendly and sustainable
  • Reduces soil erosion and flooding
  • Provides backup water supply

Feasibility

Rooftop rainwater harvesting is feasible for homes, schools, apartments, industries, and commercial buildings. The system is cost-effective and can be implemented in both urban and rural areas with moderate rainfall.
