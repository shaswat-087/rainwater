⚙️ Step-by-Step Installation Guide 🛠️

1. Prepare the Site

🔷 Identify rooftop runoff points and level the ground for tanks.

2. Assemble the Tanks

🔷 Connect modular tanks and seal joints with waterproof gaskets.

3. Install Filtration Unit

🔷 Add sediment and charcoal filters for clean water.

4. Connect the Pipelines

🔷 Route pipes to tanks and overflow drainage safely.

5. Integrate Usage

🔷 Connect outlets to garden, washing, or toilet systems.
